Abel, Makkonen Tesfaye, a pop star known as The Weeknd, is an R&B record producer, singer and songwriter from Toronto. You may hear his best-known song, “Blinding Lights,” a song produced through his record label, XO and Republic Records, on his fourth studio album “After Hours” in 2020, on the radio. You may have also heard his other famous songs such as, “Starboy,” or, “I Feel It Coming.”
